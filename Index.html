WELCOME TO SAROVA ZAWADI RESTAURANTS

A fusion of flavors, delicately balancing sweet & savory elements in artful presentations captivates the eyes before the palate. Such is the allure of Thai cuisine & rich Pan-Asian delicacies embraced with open arms in the vibrant heart of Kenya.
Our appreciation for this nuanced and layered experience inspires us to bring you, Saina by Sarova, the latest dining experience located at 102 Manyani East Road, Lavington. We celebrate the diversity & complexity of rich culinary traditions, each dish telling a story shared by both Kenyan & Thai cultures. Presenting an extraordinary dining experience, the restaurant is set amidst tranquil surroundings, harmoniously blending natural tropical aesthetics with elegant décor & design – Zen, to say the least. Private dining rooms, a garden bar & lounge, and outdoor sitting areas await every discerning guest.


At Sarova hotels, it’s not merely about the flavors - Every dish on our menu is passionately created with mastery. Aromatic curries will transport you to the bustling streets of Bangkok. The delicate flavors of our sushi will evoke the artistry of Japan. As we pay homage to this rich tapestry, we also recognize that the joy of sharing food and dining with family or friends transcends geographical boundaries. Join us as we unite diverse worlds in a celebration of gastronomic delight and mutual respect.
